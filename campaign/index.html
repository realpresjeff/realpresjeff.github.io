About Me

My name is Ashton Jefferson, and I’m here to serve the American people. With a rich heritage and vision for the future, I aim to blend colonial values with modern ideals, ensuring we protect our heritage while innovating for a better future.

As a descendant of Thomas Jefferson, I bring unique perspectives to the role of President. Together, we can build a country that is not only powerful but fair and compassionate.

As a descendant of both the colonized, the enslaved, and the founders, I understand both sides of America’s story. It’s time for policies that uplift all communities.

Campaign Issues

Here's where you’ll find the core principles that will guide my presidency:

  • Universal Basic Income (UBI): Provide every citizen with a monthly income to ensure economic security, framed as “the spoils of the new empire’s prosperity.”
  • Immigration Reform: Closing the borders, while offering temporary work visas for Latinos and Americans to work seasonally, helping our economy.
  • Recognition of Indigenous Sovereignty: Push for dual governance models, where tribal nations operate as semi-independent entities within the American Empire, mirroring the Roman model of federated provinces. Think of every American nation as a new Puerto Rico. One Empire, Many Nations.
  • Healthcare and Education for All: “A strong empire takes care of its people.” Provide universal healthcare and free public education, modeled after Indigenous community wellness systems.
  • Military and Defense: A “peace through strength” policy that bolsters national defense while avoiding unnecessary foreign conflicts. Emphasize protecting American interests globally.
