Campaign Platform

Presidential Campaign Platform

  • The American Empire: Many Republics, One Hemisphere

    Work with nations across North, Central, South America, and the Caribbean to establish agreements allowing citizens to travel freely throughout the Western Hemisphere for tourism, study, and cultural exchange.

  • Free Education

    Guarantee tuition-free education and expand vocational training programs nationwide.

  • Universal Healthcare

    Establish a universal healthcare system that guarantees free or affordable medical care for all residents.

  • Seasonal Immigration System

    Create expanded seasonal work visas so workers can legally enter the United States for temporary employment and return home after the season ends.

  • Make Racism Treason

    Retroactively revoke USA citizenship from those who commit racist hate crimes.
